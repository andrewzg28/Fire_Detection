History of us

The future to save

Nature

For years, we have witnessed long and arduous uncontrolled fire battles around the world. Some of these fires burned thousands of hectares of land and destroyed hundreds of houses and buildings. In addition, smoke from fire creates problems related to poor air quality, which poses health problems for people, not only in the vicinity of the fire, but also hundreds of kilometers away.

The purpose of the project was to design a prototype based on artificial intelligence techniques in which any type of fire can be classified, reported and monitored in real time.

Endeavor

This will allow us to prevent and alert people close to this type of disaster and thus reduce the risks in the affected communities or sectors.

To solve the problem we used the method of image analysis using neural networks Convolutivas which allowed us to establish a series of patterns by which we would filter the information (photographs) fed by the cameras of users' mobile devices, and thus the algorithm that we develop will analyze and process the data already classified and can establish whether it is a fire or not; this will allow the tool to be intuitive in the early detection of fires of any kind and thus be able to prevent any danger that this represents for the community.
