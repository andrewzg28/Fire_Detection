Our Services

Fire Report!

Report in real time any incident that is involved in the fire. By using the application only by taking a photo of the site and sending it to the system

See reports made

Each report is stored in our system where it can be viewed through the application where it shows in real time the information provided by the system. Where there is and where there was a fire.

Emergency service

Through the application is provided a record of important numbers that can be called at the time the photo is being sent to our system

Service support

We provide a support service in both the mobile application and this website to understand the purpose of our project.

System method

We used artificial intelligence methods, to classify information of the images that different users send to the systems to report a fire

Objective

The main objective of our project is to make the fires are reported immediately and can act immediately to prevent the spread of the fire. Inform users so they avoid the route where the fire is taking place.

User Profile

There is a control with the people who use the application to avoid fraudulent reports or deception.

Realized Reports

A real time fire record will be displayed on a map with the location of the device and the fires reported by other users in the system.

Emergency Service

In the application you are given a list of telephone numbers which you can call in case of emergency to send reports to the same national authorities.
